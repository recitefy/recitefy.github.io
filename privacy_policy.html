Privacy Policy

Recitefy

Effective Date: December 2, 2025

Recitefy (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data in a simple and transparent way.

1. Data Collection

Recitefy collects limited data required for core functionality such as user accounts, cloud sync, and revision features.

  • User account information (email or user ID)
  • App usage data (recitation progress, revision activity)
  • Device information for performance and crash reporting

We do not sell your data or use it for advertising purposes.

2. Permissions

  • Microphone: To record recitations
  • Storage: To save audio and app files
  • Notifications: To remind users about revision and updates

3. How We Use Data

Data is used only to provide app features such as syncing progress, improving performance, and enabling revision tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Offline Usage

Most features work offline. Data remains on your device unless you choose to sync it.

6. Children’s Privacy

Recitefy is safe for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
