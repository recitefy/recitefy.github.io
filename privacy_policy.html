Privacy Policy

At Recitefy, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting your experience while using our services.

1. Information Collection

Recitefy does not collect any personal data. We do not store, track, or share personally identifiable information of any kind.

2. Data Usage

While we do not collect personal data, the app may use local device storage to save your progress (e.g., memorization streaks or verse completion). This data stays entirely on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. Data Retention

All app-related progress is stored locally on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app. We do not retain any user data on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Recitefy does not integrate with any third-party services that collect or share user data. There are no analytics or advertising SDKs in the app.

5. Security Measures

Because Recitefy does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. However, we still follow best practices to ensure app integrity and safety.

6. Children's Privacy

Recitefy is designed for users aged 3 and above. We do not collect any personal data from users of any age, including children.

7. User Rights

You have full control over your experience. If you wish to stop using Recitefy, simply uninstall the app from your device. No further action is needed.

8. Policy Updates

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. Please review this page periodically for changes.
