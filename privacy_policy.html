Privacy Policy

At Recitefy, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting your information while using our services.

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal anonymous usage data to improve app functionality. No personal information is stored or shared.

2. Data Usage

Collected information helps us:

  • Enhance app performance
  • Personalize learning experiences
  • Maintain service security

3. Data Retention

Usage data is retained only during active app use. Uninstalling the app removes local data according to device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We employ trusted analytics providers that process anonymous data only. No personal information is shared with external parties.

5. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols, though no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Designed for ages 3+, we strictly avoid collecting information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. User Rights

You maintain full control over your experience. Stop using the app at any time through standard uninstallation processes.

8. Policy Updates

Revised policies will be posted here with updated effective dates. We recommend periodic review of this page.
