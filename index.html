Practice Modes

Musaffah (Duet) & Learn Mode

Musaffah (Duet) Mode: Feel like you're in the same room with your favorite reciters. Our real-time duet system lets you alternate verses with Qaris like Mishary Rashid or Minshawy. It trains your ear, builds confidence, and keeps you in rhythm.

Learn Mode: Ease into memorization with a stress-free flow. Listen and repeat in a structured loop. It's a chill practice zone to get the verses stuck in your head before you try reciting solo.

Tahaddi (Challenge Mode)

Gamify your Hifdh journey with timed challenges, Ayah flashbacks, and recall battles. Earn points as you test your memory — perfect for revision or just pure motivation.

Meet Ree

Ree is your chill Qur'an buddy. He's a little lazy, a bit sarcastic, and sometimes forgets where he kept his Mushaf — but he’s here to remind you to recite, celebrate your wins, and nap when you need it.

Don’t be surprised if he cheers you on when you hit a streak... or guilt-trips you when you miss one.
