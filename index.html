The Experience

Your Halqa, Every Day

A traditional revision circle — now in your pocket, with reciters you've admired your whole life.

01
🎲

Daily Reciters

Every day, 3 world-famous reciters join your circle. Not the same ones — keeps it fresh and alive.

02
🎙️

They Recite First

Your reciter reads a verse or page — beautifully, with full tajweed. You listen, follow along on the Mushaf.

03
📖

Your Turn

Now you recite. The verse is highlighted. Slide the button when you're done. Back and forth, all the way through.

04
🔄

Reshuffle Anytime

Want different reciters today? Watch a short ad and reshuffle your circle whenever you like.

Slide to Finish

A smooth slider signals the end of your turn. No buttons to fumble with mid-recitation.

World-Class Reciters
