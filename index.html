Musaffah (Duet) Mode

Feel like you're in the same room with your favorite reciters. Our real-time duet system lets you alternate verses with Qaris like Mishary Rashid or Minshawy. In Duet Mode, you take turns with your chosen reciter — one verse you, one verse them. It trains your ear, builds confidence, and helps you stay in rhythm. It's a powerful way to stay engaged and make memorization more immersive and motivating.

Learn Mode

Ease into memorization with a stress-free flow. In Learn Mode, you listen and repeat along with reciters in a structured loop — no pressure, no grading. It's a chill practice zone that gets the verses stuck in your head before you try reciting solo. While it's not a substitute for a qualified teacher, it’s the perfect warm-up to make your actual learning stick.

Tahaddi(Challenge Mode)

Gamify your Hifdh journey with timed challenges, Ayah flashbacks, and recall battles. Earn points as you test your memory — perfect for revision or just pure motivation.
