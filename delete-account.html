Delete Your Account

Recitefy

We respect your privacy. If you want to delete your Recitefy account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or follow the steps below.

1. Delete via App

Open Recitefy → Go to Settings → Account → Tap Delete Account. This will permanently remove:

  • Your user account
  • Your recitation progress
  • Your revision data
  • Any stored profile information

2. What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, all your personal data stored in our system is permanently removed, including data stored in Firebase Authentication and database services.

3. Data Retention

Some data may be temporarily retained in backups for security and recovery purposes, but it will be automatically deleted within a limited period.

4. Need Help?

If you have issues deleting your account, contact us at: recitefy@gmail.com
